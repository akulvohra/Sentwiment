What is Sentwimnet?

Sentwiment is an open source natural language processing tool for Twitter. The project uses machine learning modules to process various tweets for a certain topic to analyze Twitters sentiments toward it whether it be positve, negative and nuetralness. The model uses a method called indexing to determine its polarity and subjectivity. Polarity tells us what the opinion on the topic is (positive, negative, nuetral). Subjectivity tells us how much of an opinion the tweet is.
